About The Organization

Our History

Established in November 2025, GreenWave Organization is a youth-led initiative dedicated to fostering environmental conservation and uplifting communities through meaningful social work.

Guided by the leadership of President Sasindu Nanayakkara, our organization strives to create sustainable, long-lasting change through education, awareness, and action.

Our journey began with an impactful first event an online educational lecture on "Biotechnological Tools for Plant Conservation", conducted in collaboration with the Education and Awareness Unit of the Western Province Central Environmental Authority.

This initiative marked the beginning of our commitment to empowering students and the public with knowledge that supports environmental protection.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect the environment and support our communities through meaningful action. We work to educate, inspire, and encourage people—especially young minds—to care for nature, help one another, and take part in building a sustainable future for all.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every person feels responsible for the planet and the people around them. As a youth-led organization, we aim to grow into a strong, trusted movement that leads positive change, creates greener spaces, and brings hope and support to communities across Sri Lanka.

Our Values

We value knowledge and believe education is the first step toward change. Through workshops, talks, and awareness programs, we help people understand the importance of caring for the environment and each other.

What We Do

Environmental Conservation

From tree planting initiatives to habitat restoration, we actively work to protect and restore natural ecosystems.

Educational Programs

We organize lectures, workshops, and training sessions to educate students and the public on environmental protection and sustainable practices.

Community Cleanups

Regular cleanup drives in parks, beaches, and waterways to keep our environment free from litter and pollution.

Social Initiatives

Compassionate projects aimed at uplifting communities and creating positive social impact alongside environmental work.

Resource Mobilization

Facilitating donations and mobilizing resources to support our conservation and community projects.

Volunteer Engagement

Empowering volunteers of all ages and skill levels to participate in meaningful environmental and social work.

About Us

Separated by distance but connected by vision, the youth of GreenWave gather right at the frontlines of the environmental crisis.

They do not just observe the changing tides; they are the current. In this large ocean of challenges, they build the bridges of education and community care, ensuring that no call for help goes unanswered and no leaf is left unturned.
